Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our English learning platform

1

How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2

Is this website free to use?

We offer both free and premium content. Basic lessons and exercises are available for free, while advanced courses and personalized features require a subscription.

3

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, creating an account allows us to track your progress, save your completed lessons, and provide personalized recommendations based on your learning needs.

4

How can I build my English vocabulary?

We offer vocabulary-building exercises, flashcards, spaced repetition tools, and contextual learning activities to help you expand your English vocabulary effectively.

5

Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our premium courses. These certificates can be downloaded and shared on professional networks like LinkedIn.
